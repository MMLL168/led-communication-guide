Understanding LED Indicators

LED indicators are a universal language used across technology devices to communicate status, alerts, and system information. Understanding these patterns is crucial for troubleshooting and monitoring device health.

Power Status

Indicates whether device is on, off, or in standby mode

Operational State

Shows normal operation, processing, or idle states

Error Alerts

Warnings and critical errors requiring attention
